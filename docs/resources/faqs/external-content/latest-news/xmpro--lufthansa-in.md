<p>We’re excited to be working with <a href="https://www.lufthansa-industry-solutions.com/de-en/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Lufthansa Industry Solutions</a> at Hannover Messe this year as part of their Industrial IoT demo.</p>
<p>Our aim is to showcase how organizations can move beyond sensor deployment and data analysis to realizing the true value of IoT by incorporating it into your business processes.</p>
<p>To see the live demo, visit the Lufthansa Industry Solutions stand in Hall 6 row H.</p>
