<p><em>Dallas, TX, November 30, 2020</em> – XMPRO is excited to share that it has been listed as a Sample Vendor for Continuous Intelligence in the recently published Gartner Hype Cycle for Supply Chain Planning Technologies, 2020.</p>
<p>According to Gartner, “Data and analytics leaders should consider continuous intelligence for new business processes and when making significant changes to existing processes. It applies to situations in which real-time data from the last few seconds or minutes significantly improves business decisions.”</p>
<p>“We consider our inclusion in this report a validation of the importance of continuous intelligence for supply chains in an increasingly complex, fast-moving and digital world,” said Pieter van Schalkwyk, CEO of XMPRO. “XMPRO’s Low Code Application Development Platform is focused on helping companies build a digital twin of their supply chain to get real-time insights and act on critical events faster.”</p>
<p style="font-size:12px">*Gartner, Hype Cycle for Supply Chain Planning Technologies, 2012 by Tim Payne et al., 12 November 2020</p>
